Welcome to VISE

Where Innovation Meets Accessibility

Established to offer exquisite quality goods at accessible prices, VISE represents a commitment to excellence and innovation in consumer electronics.

Our Product Range

At VISE, we offer a comprehensive range of consumer electronics, including:

  • Televisions: Cutting-edge models that provide unparalleled viewing experiences.
  • Refrigerators: State-of-the-art appliances to keep your food fresh.
  • Washing Machines: Efficient and reliable machines for all your laundry needs.
  • Air Conditioners: Advanced cooling solutions for ultimate comfort.

VISE by Vijay Sales

Our Legacy – Vijay Sales

In 1967, Mr. Nanu Gupta, affectionately known as "Bade Sir," founded Vijay Sales with a vision to deliver top-quality products.

Building on this heritage, Vijay Sales introduced VISE in 2014 — a brand focused on innovation, quality, and affordability.
